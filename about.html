My Testimony

    I long for something to satisfy me and I've tried so hard to get what I wanted but
    when I have it, emptiness still remain in my heart.
I'm so worried about many things in life.
Until the day Jesus called me for his very own purpose in my life and
I accept him as my Lord and savior, everything changes.
And then later on he reminded me that 2 weeks before that day,
I desperately pray to him that I can't go on anymore and I want him to use somebody to show me who he is.


Indeed he is faithful to hear my cry.
He restore my relationship with my family, and brings healing to every sickness in my body.

I'm so grateful to where He is leading me right now. And I desire to be used by him even more from glory to glory.
 
I keep his promises deep within my heart and treasure his words in my life.
Jesus is my life and I can't live without him.

And this is my testimony that Jesus step into the darkness where I dwell in
and elevate me from the sin where I am drowning.
