About This website

The ultimate purpose of this website is to help you to go deeper
in knowing The WORD of God and live a life full of abundace.

Through this website may you come to know the truth and the truth will set you free.
It includes:
    Worship songs to praise and worship the Lord for his Goodness,
    Inspirational Message to encourage you to be strong and courageous in life.
    Awesome Bible stories to give you a lesson for a lifetime and
    Amazing Testimonies of the God's Plan is perfect and pleasing for you.
        
Always Remember that the Word of God is not just to inspire you, But to transform you.
The moment that you hear the Word of God, there must be an application.
as James 1:22(NLT) says "But don't just listen to God's word.
You must do what it says. Otherwise, you are only fooling yourselves."

There must be Change that time that you hear the word of God.
The Word of God redeemed, renewed and restored us from the power of darkness.

So may this website meet your expectation in knowing more of Him.

May the Love of God and the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ and the
fellowship of the Holy spirit be with you now and forever.

My Testimony

    I long for something to satisfy me and I've tried so hard to get what I wanted but
    when I have it, emptiness still remain in my heart.
I'm so worried about many things in life.
Until the day Jesus called me for his very own purpose in my life and
I accept him as my Lord and savior, everything changes.
And then later on he reminded me that 2 weeks before that day,
I desperately pray to him that I can't go on anymore and I want him to use somebody to show me who he is.


Indeed he is faithful to hear my cry.
He restore my relationship with my family, and brings healing to every sickness in my body.

I'm so grateful to where He is leading me right now. And I desire to be used by him even more from glory to glory.
 
I keep his promises deep within my heart and treasure his words in my life.
Jesus is my life and I can't live without him.

And this is my testimony that Jesus step into the darkness where I dwell in
and elevate me from the sin where I am drowning.
